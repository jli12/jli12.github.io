Hi! I am Jason, and this is what I'm passionate about.

The human body is such an incredible, yet so greatly underappreciated vessel. There are fellow humans out capable of amazing feats such as lifting over 1,000 pounds off of the ground with their bare hands, or running 350 miles (81 hours) straight without any rest. However, I still call it a vessel because housed inside that incredible sack of epithelial and connective tissue lies an blob of mass even more incredible and even more underappreciated – the human brain. Just stop and voice your thoughts about that for a second – wow, the fact that you can do that is crazy. Of the unquantifiable number of living things on this Earth, you are one of ~8 billion that are able to think to such a degree. And since you're reading this, I'd assume your also one who has mastered the skill of walking, through coordinating the >200 muscles in your body involved in the act, and have memorized 100,000 digits of Pi... okay maybe not that, though once again, there are fellow humans who are capable of such memorization feats. The human brain is fascinating, and I hope to be able to utilize my background in Biology as well as Computer Science to uncover more of its secrets in the future.
